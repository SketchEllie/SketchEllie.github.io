HOW TO USE A/V TUTORIAL GUIDES

Software/Materials Used to Make The Project: Premiere Pro, After Effects, Canon T6 Rebel

How-to tutorial guides on how to use the A/V equipment in specific classrooms throughout the NYU Tandon School of Engineering building.



Often times, student and faculty alike are unsure on how to use the A/V system installed in classrooms. These videos are a means to explain the basics of the system.
Together with my co-workers at NYU Media Services, we produced a script that would contain the necessary information needed to operate the classroom systems. I took charge of filming and editing process of the videos while my peers worked on the audio portion.
