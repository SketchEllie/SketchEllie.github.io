A BIT ABOUT ME

My name is Ellie and I am an Asian-American artist from NYC with a focus on storytelling through visual mediums.

I strongly believe that narrative is one of the most powerful tools you can use in order to connect with your audience and share your message with them. Everything has a story behind it, how it's come to be, its purpose in life and the journey to come. There's so much to learn from the world and I want to be a part of telling those stories.

When you hear storytelling you usually think of verbal or written stories. I find that using visuals add another dimension to the storytelling experience. Whether it's through illustrations or comics, live action films or animations, I want to utilize the visual medium to tell stories in a meaningful and memorable way.

I enjoy getting lost in the world during my past time, whether it's through enjoying other stories, exploring nature and traveling the world, or living vicariously through others in their journey. I graduated from New York University Tandon School of Engineering with a B.S. Degree in Integrated Digital Media.
